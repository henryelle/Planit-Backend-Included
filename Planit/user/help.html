TRACKER PAGE

To create a new planit, first select if it's a habit or goal. Then, select the planet theme you would like from the dropdown. Lastly, name your planit. Then, click the "create planit" button. Your planit should appear on the right.

Hover over your planit graphic for 2 seconds to see the planit name. Double-click on your planit graphic to display the notes section. Add notes corresponding to that planit. Double-click anytime to display the planit's notes.
